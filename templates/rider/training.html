Welcome to Pawfect Finds Delivery Platform

As a rider, you'll be responsible for delivering pet products to customers across your area.

Key Features:
  • Real-time Order Updates: Receive instant notifications via Socket.IO when new orders are available
  • Flexible Schedule: Accept deliveries when you're online and available
  • Earnings Tracking: Monitor your delivery earnings and performance
  • Navigation Support: Get directions to pickup and delivery locations

How to Use Your Dashboard
1. Going Online

Toggle your availability status to start receiving delivery requests. The system uses Socket.IO for real-time updates.

2. Accepting Orders
  1. Review available orders in the "Available Orders" section
  2. Check order details: items, delivery address, and payment amount
  3. Click "Accept Order" to confirm the delivery
  4. Navigate to the seller location for pickup
3. Managing Deliveries
  • Update delivery status: "Picked Up" → "On the Way" → "Delivered"
  • Communicate with customers if needed
  • Take photos as proof of delivery

Standard Operating Procedures
Pickup Process:
  1. Arrive at seller location on time
  2. Verify order details and items
  3. Handle products with care (especially fragile pet items)
  4. Confirm pickup in the app
Delivery Process:
  1. Contact customer if you're running late
  2. Handle packages carefully during transit
  3. Verify delivery address before handing over items
  4. Collect payment if COD (Cash on Delivery)
  5. Mark delivery as completed in the system
Safety Guidelines:
  • Always wear your rider identification
  • Follow traffic rules and drive safely
  • Keep your vehicle well-maintained
  • Report any incidents immediately

Providing Excellent Service
Professional Conduct:
  • Be polite and respectful to all customers
  • Dress appropriately and maintain good hygiene
  • Communicate clearly and professionally
  • Handle complaints with patience
Handling Special Situations:
  • Customer not available: Contact them via phone/message
  • Wrong address: Verify with customer before proceeding
  • Damaged items: Report immediately, don't complete delivery
  • Payment issues: Contact support for assistance
Important: Your behavior reflects on the entire platform. Maintain professionalism at all times.

Maximizing Your Earnings
How You Earn:
  • Base delivery fee per order
  • Distance-based compensation
  • Bonuses for on-time deliveries
  • Performance incentives
Performance Metrics:
  • On-time Delivery Rate: Maintain 95%+ for bonuses
  • Customer Rating: Aim for 4.5+ stars
  • Acceptance Rate: Higher rates get priority access
  • Completion Rate: Don't cancel accepted orders
Tips for Success:
  • Be online during peak hours
  • Accept orders promptly
  • Plan efficient routes
  • Maintain good customer reviews
